Introducing our amazing staff!

Craig Pelton
Pelton is the Dean of Greendale Community College. He is a graduate of Appatomax University and holds a Bachelors of Educations degree. Pelton has worked in Scholastics for over 10 years although his résumé could be seen as a poor reflection of this since all his previous places of employment have since shut down.

Francesca "Frankie" Dart
Frankie is a consultant hired to help improve Greendale. She may look like the lady from the IT department but thats just a coincidence that they look alike. Please stop asking. Unless you need help with your time at Greendale, then please reach out to Ms. Dart.

Ben Franklin Chang
Once the spanish instructor, turned student, turned security gaurd, turned dictator, turned amnesiac, turned student again. Ben has had a long and rather confusing history here at Greendale but we do appreciate his teaching abilities.

Vice Dean Robert Laybourne
head of Greendale's air conditioner repair annex and the real power behind Greendale. When Dean Pelton decides to confront Laybourne over the annex purchasing an espresso machine, Laybourne shows him that the air conditioning annex is the primary source of Greendale's funding. He thus holds complete power over the dean's office.

Jerry Minor
Head janitor at Greendale. We dont know much about him but he loves cleaning up paint.
